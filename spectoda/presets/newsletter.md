## Brief Template

## Guidelines

- One clear theme per send
- Deliver specific relevance for readers quickly
- Practical result first, then technical detail
- Natural invitation to continue conversation
- Structure: subject line, preview text, greeting, main section (2-3 paragraphs), CTA, sign-off
- Target word count: 300-600 words
- Subject line: max 50 characters, clear and specific
